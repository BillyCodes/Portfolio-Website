About
Who Am I?

Hi, My Name Is Billy Collins. I am an aspiring Software Engineer and Writer. In 2018 I took Computer Science courses in college while learning C++ and other CS related courses. In 2021, while out of school I started studying and learning Front End Web Development more seriously through self study and found that I genuiely love and enjoy it.


Through diving into and exploring the world of technology and figuring what I'm passionate about I've discovered that outside of myself I want to inspirire other people to find their own way into the world of technology. I want to use my other interests, such as writing, storytelling, books, animation and cinema to be a vehicle to accomplish this. Outside of just Computer Science and coding I want to become an Ambassador for Stem, bringing light to other areas of S.T.E.M such as robotics and science.


I am inspired by youtube shows like CrashCourse and SchShow. I loved seeing how other media organziations have been used to spread education like Disney, Marvel, PBS, Cartoon Network and others. I will one day create my own platform in that likeness as well. Ultimately, I want to combine education with artistic avenues so that learnig can be filled with amazement, wonder, and exploration.
